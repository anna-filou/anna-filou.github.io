My aim is always to get the info and message across to the viewer in the most efficient amount of time. That's because people tend to get frustrated and leave if they don't find what they're looking for right away.

For that reason it's important to make navigation straight forward and help people find what they came for.

This way the visitors will know that you care and value their time.
